# Customize Your Skills

These skills were built as starting points. They work out of the box, but they'll work 10x better once you customize them to YOU.

## How to Edit Any Skill

1. Open Cowork → Customize → Skills
2. Find the skill you want to edit
3. Click the three dots → Edit with Claude
4. Tell Claude what to change in plain English

## What to Customize

Here are the highest-impact changes for each skill:

- **Morning Briefing** — Add your industry, the news sources you care about, and your time zone.
- **Meeting Prep** — Tell it which calendar app you use and how formal your meetings are.
- **Hook Generator** — Add your niche and audience so hooks are tailored to your content.
- **Inbox Sweep** — Set your priority rules (e.g., "clients are always urgent, newsletters can wait").
- **Receipt Scanner** — Rename spending categories to match your actual budget.
- **Proposal Generator** — Add your business name, services, and standard payment terms.
- **Slide Deck Builder** — Add your brand colors, fonts, and logo preferences.
- **Email Drafter** — Add your name, sign-off style, and tone preferences.
- **LinkedIn Post Writer** — Add your industry, job title, and writing voice.
- **Weekly Planner** — Add your work hours, recurring meetings, and priorities.
- **Blog from Video** — Add your blog's tone, target audience, and formatting preferences.

## The Golden Rule

The more context you give a skill, the less you have to repeat yourself. Spend 2 minutes customizing now, save hours later.
